### License
We have default licenses for all things in the lab.  
You may want to [view our licenses](/licenses/License.md) if a project hasn't declared its license.  

### Start Contributing
We welcome everyone who is warm-hearted to contribute to any of its repositories.  
The contributions can be of any forms, including report a bug, suggest an idea and so on. To make your experience delightful, please read the [Contributing Guideline](/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md) and our [Code of Conduct](/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).  

### Join us
Katorly Lab is a welcoming and inclusive community. We are looking for developers who are inquisitive, innovative and persevere, and wanting to make contributions to the world together.  

To make a join request, please refer to our [Join us Page](https://katorlys.github.io/join.html). Join us and start projects or make contributions more conveniently today!  

### Get in touch
We'd like to hear from you. You can always get in touch with us through various forms. See contact details in our [Contact Page](https://katorlys.github.io/contact.html).
